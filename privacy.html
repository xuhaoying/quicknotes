QuickNote Web Clipper Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Overview

QuickNote Web Clipper is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates entirely locally on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect any personal information. All notes and data are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not have access to your data, and no data is sent to any external servers.

Permissions Used

  • storage: Used to store your notes locally on your device.
  • activeTab: Used to access the current tab's content only when you select text to create a note.

Data Storage

All notes are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. The data is only accessible on your device and is not synchronized with any external servers.

Data Sharing

This extension does not share any data with third parties. All data remains on your device and is only accessible to you.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.
